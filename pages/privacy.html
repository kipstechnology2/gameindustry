Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Kips Game Studio ("we", "us", "our") handles information when you use our HTML5 game portal (the "Service") on web or via the Games Studio mobile application.

1. Information We Process

The Service is designed to be played without an account. We process the minimum data needed for it to work:

  • Local game data — highscores, unlocked levels, in-game coins, audio and language preferences. This is stored exclusively on your device using LocalStorage and IndexedDB. We never upload it.
  • Technical data — IP address, browser type, device type, and crash reports collected by our hosting provider for security and reliability.
  • Advertising data — handled by our ad partners (see Section 4).

2. How We Use Information

  • To deliver and improve the Service.
  • To remember your settings and progress on your device.
  • To detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, or technical issues.
  • To comply with applicable law.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

We use the browser's LocalStorage, IndexedDB, and Service Worker cache to make the Service work offline and to remember your preferences. These are not third-party cookies and are not used to track you across websites.

4. Advertising

We display advertising provided by Google AdSense (web) and Google AdMob (mobile app). These services may use cookies, advertising identifiers, or local storage to serve personalized ads. You can review and adjust your choices via:

  • Google Ads Privacy
  • Your Online Choices (EU)

5. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

6. Data Retention

Local game data remains on your device until you clear it via your browser settings or via the in-app "Clear cache" action.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g. GDPR, CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, or delete the data we hold about you. Because we do not maintain a central user database, most rights can be exercised directly by clearing your local browser storage. For any other request, contact us using the email below.

8. Changes

We may update this Policy. Material changes will be highlighted on this page.

9. Contact

Questions? Email support@kipstechnology.com.
